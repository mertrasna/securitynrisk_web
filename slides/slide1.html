Slide 1: An Introduction to Security

Confidentiality

  1. Keeping information secret.
  2. Only authorized people should see the information.
Example: Your password must only be known to you, not to random people.

Integrity

  1. Making sure information is accurate and not changed by accident or bad people.
Example: Imagine you transfer $100 to a friend. If the amount changes to $1000 by a hacker, that‚Äôs a problem. Would break Integrity.

Availability

These three together are called the "CIA" Triad of security.

Malware - Core Concepts

What is Malware?

  • Malicious software designed to damage or disable computers and systems.
  • Types: Virus, Worm, Trojan horse, Ransomware, Spyware, Rootkits, Keyloggers.

Common Infection Vectors

  • Phishing emails and malicious attachments.
  • Infected websites and downloads.
  • USB devices and removable media.

Protection Measures

  • Antivirus and Anti-malware tools.
  • Regular software updates and patches.
  • User education and awareness.
  • Firewalls and network monitoring tools.
